About Akshaya Centre Tirurangadi

Your local gateway to Kerala’s flagship e-governance initiative — delivering transparent, efficient, and citizen-centric digital services.

What is Akshaya?

Akshaya is a pioneering e-governance project launched by the Government of Kerala in 2002 — the first of its kind in India to achieve mass digital empowerment. Starting as an e-literacy initiative in Malappuram, it has grown into a statewide network of centres that bridge the digital divide.

Today, Akshaya Centres serve as multi-purpose hubs delivering Government-to-Citizen (G2C) services, financial inclusion, skill development, and more — right at the community level.

Our Role in Tirurangadi

At Akshaya Centre Tirurangadi, we bring these services closer to you — making government procedures faster, more accessible, and hassle-free. From certificates and payments to online applications and digital assistance, we’re here to empower every citizen in our locality.

Vision

Achieving 100% digital and social inclusion by ‘Connecting the Unconnected’.

  • • Improve services at the base of the delivery chain
  • • Build collaborative relationships with citizens and communities
  • • Leverage government assets to enhance mission productivity

Mission

To bridge the gap between the ‘Information Rich and the Information Poor’ — transforming governance to provide efficient, transparent, and convenient services to citizens through Information & Communication Technologies.

  • • Make services accessible to the common man in their locality
  • • Improve public service delivery and e-literacy
  • • Act as agents of change for socio-economic development
